ABOUT MCST




Mission

At Mandaluyong College of Science and Technology (MCST), we aim to provide quality education in science, technology, and communication, empowering students to become innovative and socially responsible individuals.

Vision

MCST envisions being a leader in academic excellence, producing graduates who excel in their respective fields and contribute to the global community.

History

Mandaluyong College of Science and Technology (MCST) is the first higher education institution in Mandaluyong to provide free college education for local students. With state-of-the-art facilities, MCST ensures students have the resources they need to complete their studies. The college offers scholarships and free access to amenities such as the gym, library, e-library, computer labs, and more. In collaboration with the Mandaluyong City Local Government Unit (LGU), MCST also provides students with essential items like PE uniforms for their PATH-FIT courses, as well as free IDs and lanyards. MCST officially opened on September 23, 2023, and is now welcoming its second batch of enrollees.

Location

Our campus is located at Welfareville Compound, Brgy. Addition Hills, Mandaluyong, Philippines. Visit us and experience the vibrant academic and cultural environment.
