Courses We Offer

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems. It is a degree program that prepares students to design, implement, and manage technology solutions for businesses. It combines technical skills with business knowledge, equipping graduates for careers in IT management, systems analysis, and technology-driven decision-making.

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics

A Bachelor of Science in Mathematics is a degree program that focuses on developing strong analytical, problem-solving, and mathematical skills. Students learn advanced mathematical concepts and techniques, preparing them for careers in fields such as finance, data analysis, teaching, or research.

Bachelor of Arts in Communication

A Bachelor of Arts in Communication is a degree program that focuses on developing skills in effective written, verbal, and digital communication. Students study areas such as media, public relations, journalism, and interpersonal communication, preparing them for careers in media, corporate communication, marketing, and more.

About Us

Mandaluyong College of Science and Technology (MCST) is the first higher education institution in Mandaluyong to provide free college education for local students. With state-of-the-art facilities, MCST ensures students have the resources they need to complete their studies. The college offers scholarships and free access to amenities such as the gym, library, e-library, computer labs, and more. In collaboration with the Mandaluyong City Local Government Unit (LGU), MCST also provides students with essential items like PE uniforms for their PATH-FIT courses, as well as free IDs and lanyards. MCST officially opened on September 23, 2023, and is now welcoming its second batch of enrollees.
